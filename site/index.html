Features

Not a calendar.

Everything you need between meetings, living in 300 pixels of your menu bar.

01
Productivity

Focus Timer

Calendar-aware pomodoro. Sees your next meeting in 47 minutes, suggests a 40-minute deep work block. Shows in menu bar.

02
Meetings

Auto-Join

Detects Zoom, Meet, and Teams links. A green "Join" button appears 5 minutes before. One click. You're in.

03
Awareness

Conflict Detection

Overlapping meetings get flagged with a warning badge. The menu bar shows both when they're about to collide.

04
Wellness

Meeting Fatigue

Color-coded meter shows your meeting load — green to red. Smart nudges when you have 3+ back-to-back meetings.

05
Visualization

Calendar Heatmap

Days colored by meeting density. See your busiest weeks at a glance, like GitHub's contribution graph.

06
Timing

Wrap-up Alerts

Configurable notice before your meeting ends. 1, 2, 3, or 5 minutes. No more getting cut off mid-sentence.

07
Aesthetic

Time-of-Day Themes

Dawn, morning, afternoon, evening, night. The interface subtly shifts its palette to match the light outside.

08
Customization

Accent Colors

7 presets or enter any hex. Your chosen color flows through every element — calendar, buttons, toggles, progress bars.

Keyboard First
